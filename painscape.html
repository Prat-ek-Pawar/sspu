Dear Friends, it is our great pleasure to welcome you to PAINSCAPE 2026, a truly one-of-its-kind conference, with this year's theme focusing on fascial-related chronic pain, an emerging and highly relevant frontier in modern pain medicine.

PAINSCAPE 2026 marks a significant academic milestone as the 1st annual conference organized by the Society for Study of Pain, Pune. This conference reflects our vision to foster meaningful scientific dialogue, encourage innovation, and translate evolving concepts into effective clinical practice.
