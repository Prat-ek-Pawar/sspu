Dear Esteemed Members of SSPP,

Greetings from the Secretariat of SSPP!

It gives me immense pride to share that the SSPP Pune Branch stands as the ISSP city chapter with the highest number of members in the entire society, and is home to the largest concentration of pain physicians in any Indian city. This extraordinary strength is not just a statistic—it reflects Pune’s dynamic medical community and our collective commitment to advancing the field of pain medicine.

Being entrusted with the responsibility of leading this esteemed group as the Secretary of the SSPP Pune Branch is both a privilege and an honor. I am deeply grateful for the confidence placed in me, and I look forward to serving our members with dedication, transparency, and relentless enthusiasm.

Our Vision and Aspirations

As we stand at a pivotal moment in the evolution of pain medicine in India, the Pune Branch aims to set new benchmarks in professional excellence and community impact. Our goals for the coming term include:

  • 1
    Expanding Public Awareness

    Chronic pain affects countless lives, yet awareness about safe, scientific, and interventional pain management remains limited. We aim to:

    • Organize multicity public awareness drives
    • Conduct patient education camps
    • Use digital platforms to spread accurate, accessible pain-related information
    • Collaborate with NGOs, hospitals, and community groups to ensure our message reaches every corner of society
  • 2
    Strengthening Academic Excellence

    Pain medicine is evolving rapidly, and staying updated is essential. We aspire to:

    • Host regular conferences, CMEs, and hands-on workshops
    • Bring national and international experts to Pune
    • Encourage research, publications, and academic collaboration
    • Create structured training opportunities for young pain physicians, nurturing the next generation of leaders
  • 3
    Building a Stronger, United Community

    A strong branch thrives on unity. We will:

    • Promote open communication among members
    • Encourage interdisciplinary collaboration
    • Conduct social meets and interactive sessions
    • Build a supportive network where senior and junior members learn from each other
  • 4
    Setting Pune as a Model Branch

    With our size, expertise, and enthusiasm, we are uniquely positioned to make the Pune Branch a model chapter for the entire SSPP. By maintaining high professional standards, proactive engagement, and visionary initiatives, we aim to be a source of inspiration for all city branches across India.

Honouring Our Legacy

None of this would be possible without the tremendous groundwork laid by our seniors—visionaries who introduced structured pain practice, guided young physicians, and built the foundation we proudly stand on today. Their wisdom, ethics, and commitment will continue to guide every step we take. Our endeavour will be to carry their legacy forward, enhancing it with new ideas and renewed energy.

Looking Ahead

The future of pain medicine is filled with opportunities—advances in interventional therapies, regenerative medicine, neuromodulation, and evidence-based multidisciplinary care. As a branch, we will strive to stay ahead of the curve, ensuring our members are empowered with the latest knowledge and our patients receive the highest standard of care.

Together, let us strengthen our fraternity, uplift our community, and continue to shape a pain-free tomorrow for countless individuals. I look forward to a productive and inspiring journey with each one of you.

Dr Sanjog Mekewar
Secretary, SSPP Pune Branch
