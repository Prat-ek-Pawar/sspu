About SSPP / Roadmap

Strategic Plan

Goals and Objectives

SSPP Pune Branch

01

Enhance Understanding

To enhance understanding of pain among healthcare professionals and the wider community.

02

Advance Practices

Advance pain management practices through evidence-based care and innovative treatments.

03

Educate Stakeholders

Educate clinicians, researchers, patients, and policymakers about the latest developments in pain medicine.

04

Strengthen Society

Strengthen the branch by expanding membership, fostering collaborations, and building relationships.

Key Objectives

Our roadmap to achieving professional excellence

  • Promote academic and scientific initiatives in pain medicine locally and nationally.
  • Increase public awareness about pain, its impact, and effective management strategies.
  • Encourage and support research in pain and related fields to advance knowledge and innovation.
  • Organize CME programs, workshops, and hands-on training for pain physicians.
  • Conduct meetings, conferences, and interactive sessions to foster learning and collaboration.
  • Publish journals, newsletters, and digital content to disseminate current knowledge and best practices.
  • Establish guidelines and protocols for efficient and standardized pain clinic operations.
  • Build affiliations with national and international associations to promote knowledge sharing.

  • Take proactive steps to position Pune as a model chapter in the field of pain medicine.
