Membership / Benefits

Why Join SSPP?

Benefits of Membership

Join a community committed to excellence, innovation, and the future of pain medicine.

Access to Expertise

Connect with the largest concentration of pain physicians in Pune. Gain insights from senior experts, national leaders, and international specialists.

Continuous Learning

Participate in CMEs, workshops, hands-on training, conferences, and interactive sessions to stay updated with the latest advancements.

Research Opportunities

Engage in innovative research, contribute to publications, and collaborate on projects that shape the future of pain medicine in Pune.

Networking

Build strong professional relationships with peers, interdisciplinary teams, hospitals, NGOs, and policymakers in a supportive community.

Public Awareness

Take part in campaigns, patient education programs, and outreach activities that positively impact individuals living with chronic pain.

Professional Recognition

Enhance your profile and credibility by actively contributing to branch activities, conferences, and society initiatives.

Mentorship

Receive guidance from experienced pain physicians and develop leadership skills by participating in branch committees.

Exclusive Resources

Gain access to the latest guidelines, clinical protocols, research publications, and online educational materials.

Awards & Grants

Eligibility for branch, national, and international awards, research grants, and fellowships to support your growth.

Shape the Future

Play a pivotal role in creating guidelines, mentoring young physicians, and positioning Pune as a model city chapter.

Industry Collaboration

Opportunities to partner with healthcare organizations, NGOs, and government initiatives for projects and advocacy.

Ready to be part of the legacy?

Join the Society Study of Pain Pune today and elevate your professional journey.
