About SSPP / Ideology

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

We work passionately towards our vision of:

  • Elevating pain medicine as a premier superspecialty, supported by structured training, strong mentorship, and high academic standards rooted in anaesthesiology.
  • Promoting holistic, evidence-based, and multidisciplinary pain management, integrating interventional techniques, rehabilitation, pharmacology, and psychological care.
  • Fostering research, innovation, and scientific inquiry into pain mechanisms, interventional procedures, and emerging technologies.
  • Enhancing public awareness and understanding of pain management, empowering patients and reducing stigma associated with chronic pain.

Our Mission

We are committed to the following goals:

  • To advance the science and practice of pain medicine through high-quality education, structured training, and continuous professional development for clinicians.
  • To provide patient-centric, ethical, and evidence-based pain care, ensuring safe, effective, and multidisciplinary management for individuals suffering from acute and chronic pain.
  • To foster a culture of research, innovation, and scientific collaboration, encouraging studies that enhance understanding of pain mechanisms and improve clinical outcomes.
  • To raise public awareness about pain management, dispel myths, and empower patients with accurate information and accessible support.
  • To build a strong, united community of pain physicians, promoting mentorship, teamwork, and leadership within the specialty.
  • To shape pain medicine into a future-ready superspecialty, recognized nationally for its academic excellence, clinical impact, and contribution to healthcare.
